About Fortnite

Fortnite is the next big thing in the world of gaming. Offering to people of all ages, available on PC, Xbox, and Playstation. Equipped with two game modes: save the world mode and Battle Royale game mode. This game has a similar survival concept to the movie "The Hunger Games" but the terrain is in more relation to the video game "Minecraft". There are 100 players in each round; each of which are dropped from a bus flown by an air balloon. Players then pick a location on the map where they want to settle and gather materials to fight for survival. The object of the game is to be the last one standing.
