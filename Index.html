Introducing Fortnite Fortnite is the next big thing in gaming. Offered to people of all ages on PC, Xbox, and Playstation. Offering two game modes which include the save the world game mode and the most famous of all, the Battle Royale game mode in which there are 100 people on a giant map which happens to be a huge island. Everyone battles to find material and weapons to help them survive and be the last one standing. All while a storm closes in on the island forcing the remaining survivors to cam into tighter areas.
